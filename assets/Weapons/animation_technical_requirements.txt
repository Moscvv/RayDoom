- Format: PNG with transparency
- Color Depth: 32-bit RGBA
- Orientation: Facing right (+X direction in your 3D space)
- Sprite Sheet Layout:
  * Idle: 4 frames (512x512 each)
  * Run: 8 frames (512x512 each)  
  * Attack: 6 frames (768x768 each)
- Naming Convention:
  `character_{state}_{frame}.png` 
  Example: `character_attack_03.png`
